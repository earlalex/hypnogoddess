About Me

Dr. Pamela Vaden, widely known as HypnoGoddess, is a rising force in the world of entertainment, medicine, and transformation. A naturopathic doctor with additional academic experience from Harvard University, she brings a rare blend of clinical intelligence, spiritual insight, and razor-sharp wit to every stage she graces.

At just 5’1”, this powerhouse is often compared to Bo Jackson for her uncanny range—stand-up comedian, professional hypnotist, healer, and multidimensional performer. After conquering a rare form of cancer, Dr. Vaden committed herself to less invasive forms of healing.

HypnoGoddess has performed on notable stages across the nation, black women in comedy laff festival and doing street hypnosis  dazzling crowds  with her unique ability to fuse hypnosis with unfiltered, magnetic comedy. She’s hypnotized tourists on the Las Vegas Strip, performed alongside Hollywood legends like Omar Gooding, and toured with Kill Tony Golden Ticket winner Charlie Mac. Her comedy credits include working with Slink Johnson, You Know Maaacus, Tony Tripoli, Blaq Ron, and more.

She is currently on a European tour, bringing her unique blend of hypnosis and comedy to international audiences. Whether she’s commanding a comedy festival, headlining a wellness conference, or performing for corporate events and private shows, HypnoGoddess tailors every experience to both entertain and elevate.

She’s also made an impact offstage, working with young people and children in both performance and therapeutic settings. Her natural ability to engage and uplift youth has made her a favorite at schools, teen empowerment programs, and family-friendly events where transformation is needed just as much as laughter.

Known as an IconMaker, HypnoGoddess also works privately with celebrity clients—offering subconscious reprogramming, visibility coaching, and hypnotic transformation sessions to those building empires in the public eye.

Her comedy is what some call “dope” (no pun intended)—hooking audiences from the first punchline. Her act can be clean or uncensored, customized for all ages or adults-only. Whether performing stand-up, hosting hypnotic activations, or speaking at events on topics like the quantum mechanics of hypnosis, neuropsychology, or mind-body-spirit medicine, HypnoGoddess captivates minds and hearts with unmatched brilliance.

Whether you catch her on stage, on tour, or on your favorite platform, one thing’s certain: HypnoGoddess doesn’t just perform—she leaves a mark.
