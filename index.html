About Me

Dr. Pamela Vaden, known as the HypnoGoddess, is a unique blend of healer and entertainer. As a Naturopathic Doctor and certified hypnotherapist, she utilizes all her healing modalities, including Neuro-Emotional Technique (NET), to address pain and trauma throughout the mind, body, and soul.

A cancer survivor herself, Pamela brings profound empathy and understanding to her therapeutic work, guiding clients toward self-mastery and inner peace.

But the HypnoGoddess is also a captivating force on stage. A professional stage and street hypnotist, and a hilarious stand-up comedian, she brings her "dope energy" to audiences, transforming rooms with laughter and mind-bending experiences. She hits the road to hypnotize, entertain, and blow minds—one show at a time.

Her philosophy? "Live, love, laugh, medicine." Experience the fusion of science and spirituality, healing and hilarity, and unlock your true potential with HypnoGoddess.
